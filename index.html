Manitoba Premier thinks Winnipeg could become a good CFL hubM

Manitoba Premier Brian Pallister is leaving the door open for Winnipeg to be a hub city for the CFL in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Draisaitl, MacKinnon and Panarin voted Ted Lindsay finalists

Leon Draisaitl, Nathan MacKinnon and Artemi Panarin have been voted by the NHLPA as the three finalists for the 2020 Ted Lindsay Award. This year marks the first time since 2016 that Oilers captain Connor McDavid has not been named a finalist for the award.
